## 🎥 **Technical Specifications for Videos**

### **App Store Requirements:**
- **Resolution**: 1080p (1920x1080) minimum
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or device-specific ratios
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds maximum
- **Format**: .mov or .mp4
- **File Size**: Under 500MB
- **Audio**: Optional but recommended

### **Device-Specific Ratios:**
- **iPhone 6.7"**: 1320x2868 (portrait) or 2868x1320 (landscape)
- **iPhone 6.5"**: 1242x2688 (portrait) or 2688x1242 (landscape)
- **iPhone 6.1"**: 1170x2532 (portrait) or 2532x1170 (landscape)

## 🎬 **Video Production Guide**

### **Tools Needed:**
1. **Screen Recording**: Expo/Simulator recordings
2. **Video Editing**: Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premiere, or DaVinci Resolve
3. **Motion Graphics**: After Effects for animations
4. **Audio**: Voiceover recording and music

### **Production Steps:**

#### **1. Screen Recording**
```bash
# Record app interactions on iOS Simulator
xcrun simctl boot "iPhone 14 Pro Max"
# Use QuickTime or built-in screen recording

# Record Android Emulator
# Use OBS Studio or built-in recording
```

#### **2. Asset Preparation**
- **Logo animations**: LiftLink logo with glow effects
- **UI mockups**: High-resolution interface screenshots
- **Icons**: Health, GPS, verification badges
- **Transitions**: Smooth slide and fade effects

#### **3. Editing Timeline**
```
Timeline Template (30s):
[0-2s]    Logo/Brand intro
[2-8s]    Feature 1 demonstration
[8-14s]   Feature 2 demonstration
[14-20s]  Feature 3 demonstration
[20-26s]  Benefits/Results
[26-30s]  Call to action
```

#### **4. Audio Production**
- **Background Music**: Energetic, motivational (licensed)
- **Sound Effects**: UI interaction sounds
- **Voiceover**: Professional, clear, enthusiastic
- **Audio Levels**: -6dB to -12dB for safety
